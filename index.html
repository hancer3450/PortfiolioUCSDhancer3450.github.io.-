About Me

Hello! I'm Hannah Cerros, a passionate and dedicated student currently studying at UC San Diego. I am pursuing a degree in Public Health Medicine Science, with a focus on preparing for a career as a Physician Assistant. My journey at UCSD thus far has equipped me with a solid foundation in biology, chemistry, public health, math/statistics, English writing, and computing, and I am eager to apply my knowledge in the healthcare field. With a strong commitment to patient care, I have engaged in volunteering at the hospital, working as a medical assistant, working as a research assistant, and shadowing a PA. I am moved by a desire to make a positive impact on people and am excited about the challenges/opportunities that come with being a PA. I am barely at the start of my journey, and I look forward to growing both personally and professionally towards becoming a healthcare provider.

I have skills such as: medical knowledge, critical thinking, communication skills, epathy/compassion, teamwork/collaboration, adaptability, organization, attention to detail, and problem-solving-all crucial to becoming a PA!
